Claymation
I have always been interested in movies and animation, so I tried to make a stop motion short film “The Seed”. The process involved script writing, character design, set building, frame-by frame filming, and editing— merging storytelling, visual art, and technical animation.

I didn’t have any professional equipment, so my apologies for the movie being a little wobbly. I tried my best with the resources I had at the time. After all, my aim was not to create a professional Claymation, but rather to experiment with new artistic techniques, be creative and have fun.
