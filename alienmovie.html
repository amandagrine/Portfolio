Short Film
I created a short film called "A Hard Pearl To Swallow". I co-wrote the script for the movie, played one of the main parts and edited it. The editing process involved cutting and arranging the videos, adding music and sound effects and editing sound. I also used the technique of having the same actor playing two different characters that are shown on the screen at the same time, which was quite complex and required professional setting when shooting the video, as well as overlapping the two different videos to make it seem like the characters were truly talking to each other.

The movie starts with a little girl whose mother dies of an illness, leaving the little girl all alone. But before passing away, her mother gives the little girl a mysterious pearl and an envelope that she is only supposed to open 30 years later. Meanwhile on a different planet accross the universe another story is being told of an alien girl, who struggles to find food and is close to starvation. Turns out that the little girl on Earth and the alien girl have something in common - they both need the pearl to survive.
