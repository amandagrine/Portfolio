Live Performance
I composed a piece in Ableton and performed it live using Ableton's Session View. My idea was to create a piece with an ambient, atmospheric background and a quite repetitive melody in the foreground, to keep the main focus on the different effects that would be changed and adjusted throughout the performance.

While composing the piece, I was inspired by Sampa The Great, especially her song Healing, which contains many interesting sounds and textures. I used some of her techniques in my piece as well, for instance, slowly introducing or stopping each instrument at the start or end of the song, and panning instruments to make the song more immersive.

Because I am very interested in movie sound design, another thing I wanted to do was try to create different natural sounds in Ableton (wind, raindrops, storm and thunder) from scratch. Although it took quite a while to get them to sound realistic, I really enjoyed the process. So, my piece is designed to feel like you’re listening to music in the middle of a storm. It begins with wind and a few raindrops, gradually building in intensity as the sound becomes more charged and electrified. Then lightning and thunder break through, and the energy slowly dissolves as the storm passes and everything settles again.
