Photography
Here are some photos I took and then edited in Affinity Photo, that are based on the theme of Time.

The idea of the first photo was to illustrate the feeling of being stuck in time. The image depicts how everybody else’s lives seem to be going forward, whereas the girl in the middle feels trapped, unable to find her place in the world.

Next photo represents the “power of timing”. I had this unexpected opportunity to take a photo of two cats – one painted and one real cat – who were sitting in the same position, which shows the idea of being in the right place at the right time.

The third photo was captured in a graveyard, showing tombstones of many different people who used to live here. It depicts that everyone’s time on Earth is limited, symbolizing that time is finite.
