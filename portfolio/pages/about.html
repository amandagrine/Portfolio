About me!
My name is Amanda Grine and I am passionate about art, music and video. I am from Latvia, but right now I’m studying Music, Media and Performance Technology at University of Limerick in Ireland.

I have been interested in art for as long as I can remember. In kindergarten, I was already attending art classes, which led me to enroll in an art school as an extracurricular activity. I have also participated in costume design events, where I always aim to use materials found at home. Finding creative ways to reuse everyday items is something I genuinely enjoy, and using sustainable materials is important to me. However, at the moment I'm mainly focusing on graphic design and creating art digitally.

Alongside my artistic background, I attended a secondary school that specializes in STEM subjects, and I studied higher-level math and physics, which has given me a strong technical base.

Another area that has always grabbed my attention is music and sound. I have begun exploring sound design, working mainly with Ableton and Max/MSP. Additionally, I can play multiple musical instruments, like piano, guitar and drums, and I have also sung in a choir.

On this website I am publishing various works and projects that I have created during high school, university and on my free time, which connect to art, design and sound. I wouldn’t call myself a professional in these areas yet, however I am eager to challenge myself, expand my knowledge and acquire new skills.
