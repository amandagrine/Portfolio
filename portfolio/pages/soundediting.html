Sound Editing
During Halloween season, I decided to record myself reading a short horror story. I have always loved horror movies with good jump-scares, therefore I wanted to create a similar effect, but with audio. My aim was to add sound effects and music to the story, that would allow the listener to close their eyes and feel like they were really there, right next to the main character, experiencing the same fear and terror as him. I wanted to create a spooky and scary atmosphere that would strengthen the narrative of the story.

The story that I chose was “The Cabin” by Jessica Rhoades. I tried to record some of the sounds myself, but those that I couldn’t I found on YouTube. I also used music by “Music by ErikMMusic”.

The audio piece was edited in Audacity.
