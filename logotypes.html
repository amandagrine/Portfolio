Logotypes
In Affinity Designer I created three logotypes for an imaginary record company called “Deluxe Recordings”.

For the first logo I decided to do something simple. I designed the lines of an audio spectrum and used a dark blue color for the letters to symbolize luxury.



My second logo is achromatic. I used the gradient tool for the letters and replaced the first letter of Deluxe with a pause and play button.



For the third logo I wanted to play with some colors, so I designed a CD with the shimmering rainbow effect. I wanted to make the logo even more interesting, so I illustrated the letter x with two drum sticks and used the curved text effect for the word “recordings”.
