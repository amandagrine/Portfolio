Magazine
I created a 20 page magazine designed as a printed gift in Affinity. It was inspired by the Latvian children's educational magazine "Illustretā Junioriem".

This process involved many steps. At first, I had to come up with multiple different topics for the articles in my magazine. When the draft was ready, I continued with in-depth research, gathering information and interesting facts about each topic. Then I had to combine the information from multiple sources into a coherent whole and ensure that each story has a logical flow. When the text was ready, I began creating the design. It involved photo editing, developing editorial layout, typography and illustrations, and creating print-ready production files.

This was a very interesting and unique project for me, and I improved my design skills and I gained knowledge about creating art that is meant for printing.
